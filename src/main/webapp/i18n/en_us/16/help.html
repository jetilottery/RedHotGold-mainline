Red Hot Gold

The furnace is hot, and the molten gold is flowing! Light up winning trails in Red Hot Gold! Revealed symbols are matched on the grid, leading the molten gold to big prizes! Discover the bonus and multiplier lights and pick pressure dials to win bonus prizes, and smash the gold ingot to multiply your total winnings!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

The object of Red Hot Gold is to connect symbols on the grid to form a lit trail from left to right.

Select 6 of the 9 Question Mark (?) tiles to reveal symbols, Purple Bonus light, or Green Multiplier lights. Not all selected tiles reveal lights.

Symbols on the game grid that correspond to the revealed symbol will light up. Starting from the far left of the grid, adjacent lit symbols become connected to each other.

Connect an entire trail of symbols from left to right to win the highlighted prize. Trails may connect via double-tall symbols. Up to two prizes may be won per game.

Reveal a Purple Light to enter the Bonus Picker. Select from the 8 Pressure Dials to collect up to 6 corresponding prizes. All prize values revealed are added to the total win. Reveal a COLLECT symbol to end the bonus and collect the total of the revealed prize amounts.

Reveal 3 Green Lights to trigger the Multiplier Bonus. Press START to reveal a multiplier value of 2x-5x. The bonus multiplier revealed multiplies the total combined win.

Any awarded bonus games trigger after all 6 Question Mark (?) tile picks have been selected.

After all symbols are revealed in the base game and any triggered bonuses are complete, a closing message appears, indicating the game’s outcome.

Dismiss the win message and view the game's outcome by pressing the message.

Reveal All

Press AUTO REVEAL to automatically play the base game, or the Picker Bonus to completion. Game play continues until all selections have been made.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Bonus wins are added to total winning prize values.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game's outcome.

Wins are shown in currency.
